growth timeline

tiny cow-cat → international traveler → semi-bilingual roommate

Mar 2025

born

somewhere near Tsinghua University. raining. her mom walked away, but she didn’t cry — just looked up like “now what?”

Mar 2025

found

i found her at the gate, wet and tiny. she fit in one hand and refused to stay still. we went home together.

May 2025

drawer incident

learned how to open drawers. stole her own treats. intelligence level: suspiciously high.

Aug 2025

first flight ever

Beijing → Istanbul → New York. twelve hours of silence and side-eye. customs officer said she had “a strong personality.”

Sep 2025

life in NYC

now she sits by the window on Bedford Avenue, watching pigeons and pretending not to understand English commands.

Oct 2025

learning english (sort of)

knows the word “kitten.” ignores everything else. selective bilingualism.
